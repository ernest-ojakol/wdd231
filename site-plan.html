Gorilla Trekking Site Plan

Site Name

Gorilla Trekking Uganda

The site name reflects the main activity of the site, which is trekking to see gorillas in Uganda. It is clear, straightforward, and helps with branding the site as an adventure-focused, nature-based tourism hub.

Optional Domain Availability: gorillatrekkinguganda.com

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to provide information about gorilla trekking tours in Uganda. It serves as a resource for tourists who want to explore Uganda’s wildlife and learn about gorilla conservation. The website will provide information on:

  • Available trekking tours
  • How to book a tour
  • Gorilla conservation efforts
  • Safety and guidelines for trekking
  • Frequently asked questions

Scenarios

These are the potential questions that visitors might ask on the site:

  • How can I book a gorilla trekking tour in Uganda?
  • What are the best trekking seasons for gorilla sightings in Uganda?
  • Is it safe to trek gorillas in Uganda?
  • Where can I learn about gorilla conservation efforts in Uganda?

Color Schema

The color scheme selected for the site incorporates the colors of the Ugandan flag and is meant to represent strength, adventure, and vibrancy. The colors are:

Red (#FF0000) – Used for headings and accents to represent energy and passion.
Yellow (#FFB81C) – Used for subheadings and call-to-action buttons to symbolize the sun, energy, and adventure.
Black (#000000) – Used for text and background, providing contrast and readability.

Typography

The typography selected for the site focuses on readability and impact:

  • Heading Font: Arial Black, used for all main headings (h1, h2) to make the titles bold and stand out.
  • Body Font: Arial, a clean and easy-to-read sans-serif font for the body text and paragraphs.
